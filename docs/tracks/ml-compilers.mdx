ML compilers are not just another frontend or backend story. They exist because modern workloads need richer IR design, staged lowering, schedule-aware optimization, and hardware-specific code generation strategies.

## Who This Is For

- compiler engineers moving into AI and domain-specific compilation
- LLVM readers who want to understand the next layer above raw IR
- performance engineers interested in model compilation and lowering pipelines

## Outcome

You should understand why MLIR exists, where TVM fits, and how multi-level compiler pipelines differ from classic single-IR workflows.
