This track is the core of CompilerSutra. It takes you from LLVM orientation into IR reasoning, analysis foundations, and pass-oriented thinking.

## Who This Is For

- engineers moving from compiler basics into real infrastructure
- C++ developers entering the LLVM ecosystem
- readers who want to understand SSA, dominance, and passes with production context

## Outcome

You should leave this track able to read LLVM IR, understand how LLVM is organized, and reason about why analyses and transformations are built the way they are.
